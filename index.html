About

I am a Master's Student in the IIDS Lab at KAIST EE, fortunate to be advised by Hyewon Chung. I received my B.S. in Mathematical Sciences and Electrical Engineering (Double Major) at KAIST in 2024.

My research interests are focused in leveraging mathematical tools to explain real-world phenomenons. I am particularly interested in deep learning theory, in which I work on understanding the working mechanisms of large language models.
